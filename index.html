WHAT IS DESIGN?

A design is a idea or concept to build an object or system or for the implementation of an activity, or the result of that plan or specification in the form of a prototype, product or process. Basically design inherits or reflects the taste of our style. It is the true reflection of our tradition,culture and values.In some cases, the direct construction of an object without an explicit prior plan (such as in craftwork, some engineering, coding, and graphic design) may also be considered to be a design activity.In actual the design satisfy the feelings and goal to live what we dream.A good design is the one which is not only attracted by the designer but the spectators.... moreover it should be decent, elegant and attractive and appreciated by everyone. Major examples of designs include architectural blueprints, engineering drawings, business processes, circuit diagrams, and sewing patterns. The person who produces a design is called a designer, which is a term generally used for the person who work in this field of designing, drawing, architecture, and construction. A designer should have a good aesthetic sense and taste.. moreover a designer should have enough knowledge to make the combinations of different colors and designs to make the design more attractive and appreciable.
