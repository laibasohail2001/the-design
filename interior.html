INTERIOR DESIGNING

since a long time, interior design has been observed as only making the view of a space more beautiful to the eye. Due to this, research for specific cases of how interior design can impact an audience is few. Four different journals that have all conducted different kinds of research will be included within this paper. These journals touch on different aspects of interior design that effect the end users, things such as lighting, color, scale of furniture, or even the way seating is arranged is discussed. Within all four of these journals aspects of interior design that is usually overlooked is brought to the front to help educate people about how much impact interior design can have on the people who use the spaces that makes the design.
